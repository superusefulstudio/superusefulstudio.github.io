Hey there, I'm Peter a product designer and web developer

Based in Czechia and hailing from sunny Manchester

Super Useful Studio is my own micro design agency where I offer my skills and experience to startups, business enterprises, health and fitness, churches, charities and none profits.

My skills cover:

  • Iterating design concepts quickly
  • Managing and supporting stakeholders (Clients, Executives, Users)
  • Web/app UI design
  • Product modelling (user journeys, business logic, product scoping)
  • Team leadership - Building the A-Team of design
  • Motion graphics (using AE)
  • Product design and development - from inception through to realisation
  • Web development (HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JS)
  • Coding (when necessary: NODE/NPM, PHP, JAVA, Linux, LAMP)

I have over 20 years experience and counting working for various clients, brands, agencies. I am passionate about designing good products and great user experiences.

Started my life as a Graphic Designer then went to The University of Manchester and studied Software Engineering. This gives me the edge being a designer, as I can not only code but, I understand the synergy between good design & technology. I'm like the car mechanic who can build and design cars!

I like to craft nice simple design solutions that users love to use again and again.

I love UI design and its the sweet spot for me. However my experience has seen me wear many hats. From managing other designers, working as an interim product manager, user experience designer or being a technology bridge between design, engineering and executive or stakeholders. I have a can-do-attitude to challenges and drive my energy through others in order to reach a successful result.

I enjoy working with smart, driven and motivated people. I'm fairly hyperactive so I have a work hard but play hard attitude to work and life.

I currenlty live in a spa town called Mariánské Lázně (Marienbad if your German) with the wife, two kids, a poodle and now rabbits. It's a very beautiful place to live and as a bonus we have a ski resort here so I can go snowboarding during winter. I mountain bike through the forests which are right behind our house. I am also an amateur carpenter so enjoy collecting, cutting and curing my own lumber. Nothing quite like fresh cut wood.
